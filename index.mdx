## What is zkVerify?

**zkVerify** enables **confidential browsing**: verify personal attributes or browse privately on websites **without exposing sensitive data** like emails, IPs, or credentials—using **zero-knowledge proofs** verified scalably on-chain.

You can prove claims like **“human”** or **“over 18”** without revealing underlying data, while websites get **trustworthy, chain-agnostic attestations**.

## How it works (high level)

1. A **browser extension** acts as a ZK sidecar.
2. It uses **zkTLS** (e.g. TLSNotary) to fetch authentic web data privately.
3. It runs **lightweight zkVMs** (RISC Zero or SP1 compiled to WASM) for local proof generation.
4. Proofs (Groth16, FFLONK, Boojum, etc.) are **aggregated** and sent to **zkVerify** for ultra-fast verification (**< 1 second**).
5. zkVerify produces **on-chain attestations** that sites can query via simple APIs.
